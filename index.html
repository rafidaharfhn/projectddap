ABOUT

The Katze Society is an association that cares for animals, both pets, wild animals, and animals that are in conservation such as nature reserves or zoos, in order to provide information to animal lovers and carers who want to have pets and raise funds to help their survival. animals, the Katze Society established a website with the name Katze Co which contains information about pets whose owners need help to adopt their pets or animal shelters that need media for disseminating open adopt information complete with age, pet's medical history, and previous owner's contacts If there are. The Katze Co website also provides news about the animal world which is experiencing a crisis and is in need of immediate help, as well as a petition to voice policies that are deemed harmful especially to animals
